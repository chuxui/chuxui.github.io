Wroups
Material Design Social App
  • About the project

    Wroups is a concept app to find new people with similar likes. It would be a link app to whatsapp groups.

  • Objective

    Design a simple and effective platform with Material Design and use the same styles in the desktop platform.

Mobile App

I would like to faithfully respect the Material Design Guidelines for this project but at same time make an app with personality without lose the guidelines. It have a simple layout and a colorful design that makes Wroups an atractive app.

Conclusion

I updated my knowledge in Material Design Guidelines with Wroups and enjoyed it a lot designing it. Next MD project I promise a lot of Material Motion :P
